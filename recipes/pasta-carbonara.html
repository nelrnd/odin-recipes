Pasta Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish from Rome.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bacon bits
  • Sour cream
  • Grated cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Meanwhile, brown the bacon bits in a dry frying pan
  3. When they are golden, add the sour cream and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Drain the pasta and pour it into the sauce
  5. Add a beaten egg, salt and paper
  6. Mix and serve sprinkled with cheese
